Album Redesign

For this assignment I was to choose the album Electra Heart by Marina. This album is very upbeat yet talks about some very serious topics. I sadly was not able to add all of the songs from the album onto the cover because this was a school assignment. I am very proud of this work and believe this is the best work I have made this year. I created this using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.
